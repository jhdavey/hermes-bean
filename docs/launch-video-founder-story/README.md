# Heybean Founder Story Launch Video

Production packet for the first public Heybean promotional video.

## Goal

Create a polished, cinematic founder-story launch video that helps people who live by calendars and to-do lists feel personally understood by Heybean.

The video should feel human first, product second. It should not feel like generic AI hype, a stock SaaS promo, or a feature checklist.

## Target Cut

- Format: 16:9 master video
- Length: 110-130 seconds
- Style: faceless cinematic founder story with premium motion design
- Core assets: founder voiceover, Heybean app footage, real or generated planner/workspace visuals
- Final output: high-quality MP4 rendered from a HyperFrames composition
